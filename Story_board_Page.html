Storyboard Page


Index Page:
Purpose: Serves as the homepage, providing a central hub to navigate through the other sections of the website. It gives an overview of what the website is about and introduces the visitor to the content

Professional Page:
Purpose: Highlights my professional background, including vision and mission statements, qualifications, skills, and career goals. It is designed to showcase my expertise and professional development in cybersecurity.

Personality Page:
Purpose: This page provides insight into my personal interests, character traits, and hobbies, allowing visitors to connect with them on a personal level.

Storyboard Page:
Purpose: Acts as a creative visual representation of my journey or goals. It outlines my major milestones in education or career using images or illustrations to make the content more engaging.

Reference Page:
Purpose: Lists the sources or references for any materials, research, or citations used across the website, giving credit to external information that helped shape the content.
