4.) Personality Test

I was categorized as a guardian according to keirsey Personality test

The Keirsey Personality Test offers a broad categorization of individuals into temperaments, and while it can provide useful insights, its general validity is debatable. In particular, the test may not be comprehensive enough to capture the full complexity of human personality. The Guardian category, which focuses on traits like responsibility,loyalty, and structure, aligns well with my personal characteristics, and I found the test to be a valid reflection of my tendencies. However, I recognize that its generalized nature and lack of detailed options may make it less valid for others. People have unique, multifaceted personalities, and a more in-depth approach might be needed to accurately represent everyone.
